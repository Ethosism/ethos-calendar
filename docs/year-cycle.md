# Year Cycle

The year cycle exists to keep large duties from disappearing into daily urgency.

It has three layers:

- monthly observances
- seasonal themes
- annual days

## Monthly Observances

### Truth Audit

Review one belief, plan, habit, or story against evidence.

Ask:

- What do I believe?
- What evidence supports it?
- What evidence resists it?
- What would I change if I cared more about truth than image?

### Household Review

Review shared duties, neglected maintenance, relational strain, and practical burdens.

This can be used by one person, a household, a team, or a group.

### Stewardship Review

Review money, tools, time, body, commitments, and future obligations.

The point is not austerity. The point is to ask whether entrusted resources are being used truthfully.

### Repair Day

Choose one concrete repair:

- apology
- replacement
- repayment
- clarification
- cleanup
- structural correction

Repair Day should not become symbolic. It should leave something visibly more truthful, whole, or accountable.

## Seasonal Themes

The calendar does not need fixed dates in its first version. Communities and households may adapt seasons to climate, culture, and local obligations.

| Season | Focus |
|---|---|
| Inheritance | What has been received, unfinished, damaged, or entrusted? |
| Formation | What habits, skills, disciplines, and examples should be formed? |
| Provision | What must be built, stored, funded, repaired, or shared? |
| Courage | What truth, duty, or protection requires risk? |
| Mercy | What accountability can become repair rather than mere punishment? |
| Mortality | What ends, what must be grieved, and what should be put in order? |
| Renewal | What should begin again after review, repair, and rest? |

## Annual Days

### Day of Remembrance

Remember the dead, the absent, the harmed, and the inherited.

### Day of Vows

Renew or release commitments truthfully.

### Day of Debts

Review debts owed, debts held, obligations postponed, and repairs unpaid.

### Day of Service

Give practical help where it is needed without claiming ownership of the recipient.

### Day of Future Generations

Review what present choices impose on children, descendants, institutions, and the world not yet born.
