# Code Of Conduct

Participation in this repository should preserve the same boundaries the calendar requires.

Expected conduct:

- Speak truthfully and concretely.
- Distinguish text, interpretation, experiment, and personal opinion.
- Respect privacy and limits.
- Do not pressure people to disclose private material.
- Do not claim spiritual, therapeutic, medical, legal, or institutional authority through this project.
- Correct mistakes transparently.

Unacceptable conduct:

- harassment or threats
- coercive pressure
- forced confession
- doxxing or exposure of private material
- using Ethosism or Ethra as a status hierarchy
- presenting calendar practices as a substitute for professional, legal, medical, or emergency help

Maintainers may remove or reject contributions that violate these boundaries.
